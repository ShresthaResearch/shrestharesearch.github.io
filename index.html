Shrestha Research Grant

An opportunity for undergraduate mechanical engineering students

About the Grant

The Shrestha Research Grant offers a unique opportunity for aspiring sophomore mechanical engineering students to gain hands-on research experience. This grant is designed to foster innovation and provide a platform for students to work on exciting projects, regardless of their prior research background.

Gallery

Eligibility Requirements

  • Must be a sophomore
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Declared Mechanical Engineering major
  • No previous research experience required

Program Details

  • Program Dates: August 26, 2025 - December 13, 2025
  • Program Length: 15 weeks
  • Application Deadline: August 1, 2025

Final Presentation

All grant recipients are required to present their research findings at a university-wide symposium at the end of the program. This is an excellent opportunity to showcase your work to faculty and peers.
